## The Story

This game was conceived as a vision and built entirely in a single conversation with an AI. No frameworks. No build steps. No dependencies. Just one HTML file and a dream of the deep ocean.

You are not a whale hunting fish. **You are a spirit of the ocean** — dancing through currents, shaping water, guiding the eternal cycle of energy and light.

## How to Play

**Move** to guide the whale through the deep. **Hold** to weave spirals of luminous bubbles that attract curious fish. As sea creatures dissolve into light and flow into you, you descend deeper through four mystical ocean layers — from sunlit surface to cosmic spirit realm. The whale slowly transforms, becoming more celestial with each light absorbed.

No tutorial needed. Just breathe and drift.

## The Layers

| Depth | Realm | Feel |
|-------|-------|------|
| 🌤 | **Surface** | Warm golden light, playful schools |
| 🌌 | **Twilight** | Cool blue calm, dreamlike currents |
| 🌑 | **Abyss** | Bioluminescent echoes, slow and mysterious |
| ✨ | **Spirit Realm** | Cosmic ocean, stars become fish become stars |

## What's Inside

- Canvas 2D rendering with dynamic ocean layers and light ray simulation
- Boid-inspired fish schooling with three behavioral archetypes (curious, fearful, spiritual)
- Procedural spiral bubble system with physics
- Web Audio API synthesized whale songs, ambient drones, and bubble chimes
- Particle systems for ambient atmosphere and absorption effects
- Smooth celestial whale transformation with glowing markings
- Screen shake, vignette, and bioluminescent deep-sea effects
- Full touch + mouse support
